Select Coursework

I have selected and linked eight projects I have done throughout my coursework that I am particularly proud of, and that I believe showcase my passions, interest, and the tenants of good librarianship.

HUD-LOD Project Proposal: A Linked Dataset of Hudson's History

I created a proposal to create a new linked dataset to store data regarding the history of my hometown of Hudson, Ohio. The project involved the evaluation of the many different types of dataset schemas, and planning for the best way to organize and store data regarding a city’s history.

Lantex Theater informational website 

I researched the history of the Lantex Theater, a historic theater in Texas. The project involved compiling information from a multitude of sources. The outcome of the project was creating a comprehensive information website about the theater, as well as creating structured data records related to the theater.

Visit the Lantex Theater Informational Website >>

Agency and Association Report

I researched the history and goals of two international organizations: the Arab League Educational, Cultural and Scientific Organization (ALECSO) , and the Association of Caribbean University, Research and Institutional Libraries. This project gave great insights into the types, work, and challenges of information agencies around the world.

Country Profile: Librarianship in Iran

I wrote a paper on the librarianship environment in Iran. This includes library statistics on Iran’s different types of libraries, librarian education, professional organizations, and key factors that impact Iran’s libraries. 

ReminderX: Stakeholder Design Review

I created a workflow and design for a new theoretical mobile application to allow people to track tasks and remind themselves. This course and project taught the end-to-end design workflow, and how to optimize the experience for all types of users.

Effective Programming in Library Cafés: Research Proposal

Library Cafes provide unique opportunities to attract and engage patrons. With this essay, I synthesized existing research about the use of library cafes in libraries and effectiveness. I then constructed a research plan to study and produce qualitative and quantitative data about the effectiveness and impact of cafes on a library’s patrons and their use of library resources.

Sample Bookstore Database

With this project, I designed a normalized database to store data for a theoretical bookstore. This project gave great insight into the challenges of effective and organized data storage.

Young Library Patrons: Information Needs

With this project, I synthesized research on young library patrons, including their biggest challenges and needs. Young patron needs include information, but also other services, including consoling. This paper also includes some of my own recommendations on how best to serve young patrons as a result of my research. Libraries serve patrons of all ages, and keeping up with the needs of new generations is vital.
